Reviews

Jane G.

So tasty! You've gotta try this! I've been telling all my friends to come try it. Hands down my favorite French restaurant in Vientiane. The owner is the head chef, and their pudding I could eat it every single day!

Roberto S.

We had a wonderful dinner at You Na. The food was exceptionally good, a highlight of our entire trip in Southeast Asia. The chef uses fresh, locally sourced ingredients. We enjoyed dishes from the weekly menu, which were very reasonably priced. The staff and owner were super attentive and friendly. I would definitely go back the next time we are in Vientiane.

Ibo R.

I had an incredible dining experience here. The food was authentic and truly stood out. The presentation was also impressive, with great attention to detail. What really made the visit even more memorable was the chef, who was a genuinely kind and welcoming person. You can tell he cares deeply about his craft. On top of that, the quality-to-price ratio was outstanding. This place is a hidden gem.
